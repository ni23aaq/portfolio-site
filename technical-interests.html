Web Development

Creating modern, accessible, and user-friendly websites is one of my key passions. I enjoy building web applications using only vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, focusing on clean design and responsive layouts.

I follow WCAG accessibility guidelines and incorporate usability principles from Nielsen's heuristics to ensure my sites are easy to use and inclusive for all users.

Cybersecurity & Ethical Hacking

Through the TryHackMe platform, I have gained foundational knowledge in networking, operating systems, and penetration testing. I have completed the “Pre Security” path and am currently pursuing the Red Team track.

Security is critical in today’s digital world, and I’m fascinated by how vulnerabilities are found and responsibly addressed to protect systems and data.
